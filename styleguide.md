## 7ï¸âƒ£ Content & Writing Style

### Voice
- Objective, precise, evidence-based
- Claims should be measurable or referenced
- Avoid exaggeration

### Headlines
- Clearly state *what you do* and *who it helps*
- Avoid vague slogans

### Technical Pages
- Step-by-step explanations
- Diagrams encouraged
- Cite research or validation where possible
